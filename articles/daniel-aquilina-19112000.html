cormac.boomer

From: Daniel J. Aquilina (dja@mail.global.net.mt)
To: (cormac.boomer@ntlworld.com); (ndon4444@aol.com); Boomer Mandy (MD6056@aol.com)
Sent: 19 November 2000 20:16
Attach: P.Coyle.doc
Subject: Padraig Coyle

Attached please find my fax dated 10.11.00 to Padraig Coyle and subsequent email message following the publication of the article. This is just for your info
Daniei

Dear Mr. Coyle.

I have read the article that you wrote and published regarding the disappearance of my brother and the other passengers allegedly on board the Piper Lance back in December 1995. Whilst renewing my thanks for your interest in the case, allow me to point out that I am disappointed.

I am not disappointed with regards to the contents of the article at all. I think it is a well written piece nor withstanding this, I am upset due to the fact that you totally ignored my fax message transmitted to you last week (dated 1Oth November 2000) wherein I specifically asked you to send me the prepared text prior to publication. I also specifically asked vou not to mention the medium/psychic which is without any shadow of doubt a very personal aspect of our tragedy as families desperate for information of our loved ones who are still missing.

Yours sincerely.
Daniel Aquilina
