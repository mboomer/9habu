Letter To Jim Nicholson, no response from Foreign Office

36 Riverdale Park North,
Belfast BT11 9DL

16 February, 2001

Mr. Jim Nicholson, MEP.,
European Office
3 Glengall Street,
Belfast BT12 5AE.

Dear Mr. Nicholson,

We acknowledge receipt of and thank you for your letter of 15th inst.

At this time we and the Williams family are pursuing this matter through our respective Foreign Offices as you suggest but to date we have had no response.

We thank you for your interest and support and will advise of any further developments at this end.

Yours sincerely,

Cormac Boomer
