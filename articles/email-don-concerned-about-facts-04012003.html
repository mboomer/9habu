Cecilia and Cormac,

I really need your help with this and I am concerned that the information I was given may not be accurate and, therefore, the whole premise around which my article to Magill is founded, could be easily rubbished. I cannot allow this to happen. So, l will need absolute conﬁrmation that the information l have is accurate.

The whole premise of the Bartolo theory is that he was, wittingly or not. irresponsibly fingered by Kullhadd on 5th November by their showing one of his aircraft. Not necessarily 9H-ABU but an Excelair aircraft.

That's what the document ‘Piper Lance Mystery - Sequence of Events‘ clearly states:

"...The journal [KullHadd] also printed a photograph of the Excelair aircraft in the same write up..."

If this is not so then it substantially weakens the foundation of the argument since the pilot could have been any private pilot in Malta and not necessarily Bartolo. l think Cecilia, you may need to speak with Daniel and Patrick to see if they can help you.

Your sentence:

"the newspaper shows picture of private plane but not clear whether it was the 9HABU and picture not clear shows a lot of clouds"

may very well be an Excelair aircraft. if so, well and good. just need confirmation

I hope you understand l am not being awkward. l am being careful and cautious. A fundamental error like this would have the case rubbished and dismissed. We cant afford to let that happen at this stage.

Sincerely,

Don

PS. The full translation is very anxiously awaited. if you can put it on an email it would be even better than fax.
