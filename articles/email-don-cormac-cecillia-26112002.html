Cecilia,

Thank you for your emails and offers of help. I ask you to treat this email in strict confidence.

Cormac has probably told you about my growing suspicion. l believe the protest held outside the Maltese Embassy in Tripoli a day or two after the assassination of Shqaqi is key. I need to get as much information on the protest as possible. What date did it take place? Who precisely organised the protest? How many attended? What threats were shouted? What exactly was contained in the protest letter that was handed into the Embassy by the protesters? What were the links between the protesters and Shqaqi‘s Islamic Jihad?

We must then try to gauge the threat assessment which the Maltese authorities undertook. Did they consider the threats made by the protesters to be real and potentially life threatening to Maltese citizens on the island or to their diplomatic staff in Libya? If so, what were the options they may have considered by way of protection and security?

I am highly suspicious of the motives behind the unsubstantiated and uncorroborated article which appeared on the front-page of 'KullHadd' on 5 November 1995, followed by a similar article on the front-page of the Maltese Times on 9 November 1995. Both suggest that Shqaqi's assassins escaped the island on a Maltese private plane. Reprehensibly, the KullHadd carried a picture of an Excelair aircraft without any attempt to conceal the company's identity. Why? Why was the KullHadd article essentially reinforced in the Times of Malta four days later?

One cannot rule out the possibility that in an effort to appease Islamic fundamentalists who were vying for revenge and making dangerous threats against Malta, a decision may have been made to offer them a sacriﬁcial lamb. If so, was that lamb pilot Carmel Bartolo?

My suspicions are reinforced by information I have concerning the Mussad operation to assassinate Shqaqi. It would appear the assassins arrived by high speed boat from Sicily and returned that way before flying back to Tel Aviv. Where precisely was their motorcycle found on the island? Was it close to the airport?

In addition to the above questions I need a very clear copy ofthe front-page of KullHadd of 5 Nov 1995. I also need a very clear and precise translation of the entire article together with the captions under the three photographs which appear. There is also a short article about the assassination of Israeli Prime Minister Rabin, and I'd like to have a translation of this also.

I can't, of course, prove any of this but we are entitled, nonetheless, to ask questions and seek accountability from the Maltese authorities given the national and international dimensions of the mystery. This theory might explain the apparent determination of the 'Sciberras Inquiry‘ to blame the pilot and try to convince us that Matthew and his travelling companions perished in an aviation accident.

I will have many more questions Cecilia and will deeply appreciate your help. But, for now, these will be most helpful.

Sincerely,

Don Mullan.
