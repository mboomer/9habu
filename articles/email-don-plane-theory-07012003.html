Informed sources state that the theory that they escaped by plane has been set aside. 2. [b]Shqaqi visits to Malta over the last three years Fathi Shqaqi ﬁrst visited Malta on the 14th December 1993. As soon as he arrived he proceeded straight to the Valletta Harbour where he boarded the ferry to Tripoli. He returned to Malta on Christmas Eve and together with other Arabs spent three days at the Diplomat Hotel. On the 27th December 1993 he left Malta for Damascus. He re-entered Malta on June l, 1994. He arrived from Tripoli together with another person. This time he had not travelled to Libya via Malta. From the harbour he proceeded to the Holiday Inn at Tigne. He spent the night and left Malta by air on the 2 June 1994. Two months later, on August 28, Shqaqi returned to Malta by air and went to rest for a few hours at the Park Hotel in Graham Street, Sliema. He left late that same evening for Tripoli. Twelve days later he returned from Tripoli and stayed again at the Park Hotel. The next day he caught the plane to Damascus. The ﬁrst time he came to Malta this year was April 17 when he proceeded directly to harbour from the airport to catch the ferry to Tripoli. On his way back to Damascus he stopped in Malta on 27th April when he spent the night at the Park Hotel. He left Malta the following day for Rome.
