Letter to Editor of Sunday Life newspaper seeking apology for article

The Editor
Sunday Life
124 Royal Avenue
BELFAST.
BT1 lEB.

Dear Sir

Re: Sunday Life llth November 2001 — Page 36

As father of Desmond Boomer referred to in the above article I wish to register my complaint at the lack of sensitivity displayed in dealing with the disappearance of my son. As your article makes clear my son has ﬁve children and a wife. Regrettably this matter has been raised before and I attach a letter from the Editor of the BelfastTelegraph dated 19 December 2000.

Following contacts from the journalist who wrote the story I believe that the story is a sensationilisation with little factual substance. As the article intrudes into the grief of my family I await your apology.

Yours faithfully

CORMAC BOOMER
